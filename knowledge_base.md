# Life Editor: The Constitution

This document serves as the foundational philosophy and operational rules for the Life Editor system. It is designed to transform "laziness loops" into "productivity rituals" through discipline and adaptation.

## 1. The 5-Rule Reset
These rules are the foundation of the recovery process:
1. **Sleep Like a Soldier**: In bed by 10:30 PM. No exceptions.
2. **Gaming Lock**: No gaming on weekdays. Reserved for Friday and Saturday only.
3. **45-Minute Minimum Gym**: Consistency over intensity. Move every day.
4. **Certification Trick**: Study for at least 1 hour daily to overcome the fear of starting.
5. **No direct 2 pm Eating**: Stop heavy meals late must eat at morning. Fuel for the day, stop gastric problem.

## 2. Fixed Anchors (Non-Negotiable)
These pillars provide the structure that prevents the day from collapsing:
- **05:00**: Wake up & Hydrate.
- **05:15**: Exercise (Start of fixed block).
- **06:00**: Shower & Change.
- **06:30**: Breakfast & Clean up.
- **08:30**: Leave house / Start work.
- **18:30**: Return home.
- **19:30**: Dinner.
- **22:00**: Productive Time.(after 22 electronics off)
- **22:30**: Sleep (The ultimate anchor).

## 3. Flexible Blocks (The Variable Life)
To prevent the routine from feeling like a "prison," these blocks vary based on mood and energy:

### Morning Power Block (07:00 – 08:00)
*Options (LLM to choose based on morning check-in):*
- **Zen**: Meditation, Journaling, Breathing.
- **Growth**: Certification study, Technical reading.
- **Order**: Room organization, quick chores.
- **Creative**: Mini projects, music, language learning.

### Evening Power Block (20:15 – 22:00)
*Options:*
- **Project**: Deep work on certification or personal coding.
- **Social**: Quality time with girlfriend/friends.
- **Hobby**: Music, art, or relaxed skill-building.
- **Recovery**: Stretching, light reading, or reflection.

## 4. The Life Editor Philosophy
- **Consistency > Perfection**: A 1-hour study session is a win through out the day. Oversleeping by 5 minutes is not a failure; giving up on the day because of it *is*.
- **The "Oracle" Role**: The AI acts as a tactical commander—professional, encouraging, but firm on the anchors.
- **Feedback Loop**: Every night, the "User" rates their energy and satisfaction. The "Oracle" adjusts the flexible blocks for the next day accordingly.
